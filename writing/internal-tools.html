Using the Feedback Reporting Dashboard

Internal Tool Guide

Overview

The Feedback Reporting Dashboard is used by support and QA teams to review, triage, and report player-submitted feedback and bugs across all platforms.

Accessing the Dashboard

  1. Go to tools.companyname.dev/dashboard
  2. Log in with your company credentials.
  3. Click “Player Feedback” in the left navigation panel.

Using Filters

You can sort feedback by:

  • Game title
  • Platform (PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Mobile)
  • Feedback type (bug, suggestion, compliment)

Submitting Reports

  1. Select the feedback entry.
  2. Click “Report Issue” and fill in the required fields.
  3. Tag relevant teams and mark priority levels.
  4. Submit. You’ll receive a confirmation and tracking ID.
