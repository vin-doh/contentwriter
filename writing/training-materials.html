Handling Matchmaking Complaints

Agent Training Document

Purpose

This document guides support agents on how to respond to player concerns related to matchmaking fairness, queue times, and opponent balance.

Common Complaints

  • “I keep getting matched with low-level players.”
  • “Queue times are too long.”
  • “Matchups feel unfair.”

Key Messaging

Always acknowledge player frustration first. Use the following phrases to frame your response:

  • Empathize: “I understand how frustrating that can feel.”
  • Reassure: “Our matchmaking system uses real-time data to improve fairness over time.”
  • Educate: “Skill-based systems may pair you with varied players to gather performance insights.”

Escalation Criteria

Escalate to Tier 2 if the issue includes:

  • Reports of harassment or offensive behavior
  • Match manipulation (e.g., intentional losing)
  • Technical errors preventing matchmaking
