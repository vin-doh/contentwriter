Fixing Login Issues

Player Support Article

Overview

If you're having trouble logging into your account, don't worry. Follow these steps to resolve common login issues and get back into the game quickly.

1. Check Your Credentials

  • Ensure your email and password are entered correctly.
  • Watch for caps lock or accidental spaces.
  • If you’ve forgotten your password, click “Forgot Password” to reset it.

2. Confirm Server Status

Sometimes login issues are due to temporary server outages. You can check server status on our System Status Page.

3. Clear Cache and Cookies

Outdated cache or cookies can interfere with login. Try clearing them and restarting your browser or app.

4. Update Your Game Client

Make sure you're using the latest version of the game. Outdated clients may fail to connect.

Still Need Help?

If none of these steps help, contact our support team with your username, platform, and any error messages you see.
